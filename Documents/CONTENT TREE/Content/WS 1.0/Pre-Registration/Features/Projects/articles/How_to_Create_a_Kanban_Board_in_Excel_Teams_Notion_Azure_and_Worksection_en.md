## What is a Kanban Board and Why Does It Matter?

**The kanban board** is an easy and powerful project management tool for
managing tasks and tracking progress. It organizes work into columns that
represent the different stages of a process. Tasks are displayed as cards that
move between columns as they progress. This gives teams a clear and visual way
to monitor workflow.

  

One of the things that makes Kanban boards so effective is their ability **to
break down complex projects** into manageable steps. Teams can easily identify
bottlenecks, have better resource allocation and meet deadlines by showing the
status of each task at a glance. They also help teams **adapt to change and
prioritize work** efficiently, making them a key part of agile workflows and
scrum methodology.

  

Kanban boards are incredibly versatile and have applications **across a range
of industries,** from IT to marketing and manufacturing. Their blend of
simplicity and detailed tracking make them essential productivity and
collaboration tools in any project workplace.

## How to Create a Kanban Board in Excel

Excel is a familiar and versatile tool. It can be adapted to create a simple
Kanban board for project management. To create your own Kanban board in Excel,
follow these steps:

### Step 1: Prepare Your Excel Workbook

  1. **Open Excel** and start a blank workbook. 
  2. Create a new sheet using the **" +"** button and rename it to **" Board".**

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_9.@x1_2.png)

### Step 2: Create Workflow Columns

  1. **Create a header row** at the top of the sheet to label the workflow stages, such as "To Do", "In Progress" and "Done".
  2. **Adjust the column widths** to provide enough space for task details. 
  3. **Use a different color** for each of the column headers to visually differentiate between the workflow stages.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_10.@x1.png)

### Step 3: Design Kanban Cards​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

  1. ​​Add new sheet through **" +"** button and name it **“Cards”.** Here you can create your cards template. 
  2. **Create the outline** of the first card template by selecting the area and using the “Fill” tool.
  3. **Include key task details** like priority, status, and assignee.
  4. **Use conditional formatting** to highlight overdue tasks or high-priority items, ensuring that critical information stands out.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_11.@x1.png)

### ​​​​​​Step 4: Start Using the Kanban Board​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

  1. As tasks progress, move them between columns by **cutting and pasting rows.**
  2. **Add filters or sorting options** to focus on specific categories or team members.
  3. **Regularly update the board** to keep it relevant and reflective of the project’s progress.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

### Advantages and Disadvantages of Using Excel for Kanban Boards

## How to Create a Kanban Board in Microsoft Teams

Creating a Kanban board within Microsoft Teams enhances task visualization and
team collaboration by integrating with Microsoft Planner. Follow these steps
to set up your board:

### Step 1: Set Up a Planner Board in Teams

  1. Open Microsoft Teams and go to the channel where you want to set up the Kanban board and **add a Planner Tab.**
  2. Click the **" +"** button at the top of the channel to add a new tab.
  3. **Select** "Tasks by Planner and To Do" from the app list.
  4. **Choose** "Create a new plan" or select an existing one.
  5. Enter a descriptive name for the plan, then click **" Save"** to finalize the setup.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_12.@x1.png)

#### ​​​Step 2: Add Columns and Cards​​​

  1. **Define workflow stages** like "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done" by adding buckets within Planner.
  2. Click** "Add task"** under the appropriate bucket.
  3. **Enter task details** such as the name, due date, and assigned team members.
  4. Press** "Add Task"** to save each card.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_13.@x1.png)

### ​​​Step 3: Assign Tasks and Collaborate​​

  1. Use the **" Assign"** option to allocate tasks to team members.
  2. Utilize the **" Comments"** section in task cards for discussions.
  3. **Attach relevant files** directly to tasks for easy access.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_14.@x1.png)

### ​​​Step 4: Monitor Progress

  1. **Drag and drop task cards** between buckets as they progress through different workflow stages.
  2. **Access the "Charts" **view in Planner to visualize task statuses and team productivity metrics through graphs and summaries.​​​

## How to Create a Kanban Board in Notion

Creating a Kanban board in Notion allows for flexible and visually engaging
project management. Follow these steps to set up and customize your board:

### Step 1: Create a New Page

  1. **Open the Notion app** or visit the website via your browser.
  2. In the sidebar, click **" Add a page"** to create a new workspace.
  3. **Give the page a relevant title,** such as "Project Tasks" or "Team Workflow", to clearly define its purpose.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_16.@x1.png)

### Step 2: Add a Kanban View

  1. On the new page, type **/Board view** on any page and press enter.
  2. Notion will **include default columns** like "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done." Rename these columns to align with your specific workflow by clicking on their headers.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_17.@x1.png)

### Step 3: Customize Columns and Cards

  1. Under each column, **click "New" **to create a task card. Enter the task title and press "Enter" to save it.
  2. Open a task card to **add details such** as descriptions, checklists, deadlines, and assignee names.
  3. Use the **" Add a property" **option to include fields like priority, tags, or additional deadlines for better task and milestone tracking.​

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_18.@x1.png)

### Step 4: Embed Widgets and Tools

  1. **Type "/embed"** to add tools such as calendars, documents, or external links for added context.
  2. **Connect the Kanban board** to related project timelines, subpages, or knowledge bases to create a comprehensive project management ecosystem.​

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_19.@x1.png)

## How to Create a Kanban Board in Azure DevOps

Setting up a Kanban board in Azure DevOps enables teams to visualize
workflows, manage tasks efficiently, and enhance collaboration. Follow these
steps to create and customize your Kanban board:

### Step 1: Create a New Project

  1. **Log into** your account through the Azure DevOps portal.
  2. Click **" New Project"** on the dashboard.
  3. **Enter a****project name** and an optional description.
  4. **Choose visibility:** public (accessible to all) or private (restricted access).
  5. **Select a process template** that aligns with your team's workflow.
  6. **Click "Create" **to finalize the project setup.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_20.@x1.png)

### Step 2: Configure Your Kanban Board

  1. **Navigate to "Boards" > "Boards"** within your project menu to open the default Kanban dashboard.
  2. Under the **" Columns" **section, you can add, rename, or delete columns to match your workflow stages.
  3. **Within Board settings,** specify WIP limits for each column to restrict the number of tasks in progress. 

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_21.@x1.png)

### Step 3: Add Work Items and Customize Columns

**Create Work Items.**  

  * Click **" New Work Item"** and choose a type (e.g., User Story, Task, Bug).
  * **Enter details** like the title and description, and assign it to a team member.
  * Save to **add the item** to your board.  
  
![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_22.@x1_1.png)

**Customize Cards**  

  * **Navigate to "Cards" **in Board settings to determine which fields (e.g., priority, due date) appear on each task card.​​​​​​

### Step 4: Use Advanced Analytics and Reporting

  1. Navigate to "Boards" > "Analytics" to review metrics such as cumulative flow diagrams and cycle time. These provide insights into workflow bottlenecks and performance trends.
  2. Use built-in progress reports to create customizable dashboards that track progress and highlight areas requiring attention.

![](/images/upload/How_to_create_a_kanban_board_in_Excel_Teams_Notion_Azure_Worksection_23.@x1.png)

## Comparing Kanban Board Features Across Platforms

We prepared a deep comparison of five popular options, highlighting their
strengths and weaknesses across essential features.

### Ease of Use

  * **Worksection:** User-friendly kanban solution with no need for extra setup.
  * **Excel:** Familiar and straightforward for most users, but requires manual setup and updates.
  * **Teams:** Intuitive for those already using Microsoft 365, offering quick integration with existing workflows.
  * **Notion:** Highly customizable with an easy-to-navigate interface, suitable for creative workflows.
  * **Azure:** Advanced but has a steeper learning curve, ideal for technical teams.

### ​Collaboration​

  * **​Worksection:** Provide task discussion with each task, real-time updates, and manageable client access.
  * **Excel:** Limited real-time collaboration without additional tools like OneDrive or Google Drive.
  * **Teams:** Seamless collaboration through built-in chat, file sharing, and task assignments.
  * **Notion:** Offers collaborative editing and flexible sharing, but lacks advanced permission controls.
  * **Azure:** Strong collaboration features tailored for software development teams with task dependencies and swimlanes.

  * **​ Worksection:** Provides API integrations and cross-service automation through Zapier.
  * **Excel:** Limited native integrations, but works well with other Microsoft tools.
  * **Teams:** Integrates smoothly with the Microsoft ecosystem and third-party apps.
  * **Notion:** Relies on external widgets and embeds for integrations, providing flexibility.
  * **Azure:** Deeply integrates with other Azure DevOps tools like pipelines and repositories.​​​

  * **Teams:** Limited customization within Planner but adequate for basic needs.
  * **Notion:** Exceptionally customizable for task views, properties, and aesthetics.
  * **Azure:** High level of customization for workflows, columns, and analytics.

### Advanced Analytics

  * **Worksection:** Includes built-in reporting and analytics for time tracking, task performance and budget.
  * **Excel:** Basic charts and graphs require manual setup.
  * **Teams:** Provides limited analytics through Planner’s built-in charts.
  * **Notion:** Minimal analytics unless manually configured.
  * **Azure:** Offers advanced reporting tools like cumulative flow diagrams and cycle time metrics.
