KEY MESSAGES:
• Flexible Kanban that adapts to your workflow
• Inline editing without opening tasks
• Bulk actions for efficient task management
• Multiple views: by status, by person, by day

UNIQUE VALUE PROPS:
1. Adjustable column widths for customization
2. Inline editing (assignees, deadlines, priorities) directly on board
3. Bulk selection and mass actions (Shift/Ctrl+click)
4. Hierarchy grouping — subtasks stay with parents
5. Smart search with partial matches (names, labels, assignees, descriptions)
6. Three views: by status, by person, by day
7. SPA technology — instant updates without page reload
8. Advanced filters with saved combinations
9. Clean, minimalist design
10. Connected to lists, Gantt, and calendar views
